General Information

The human brain is considered to be the most complex object in the known universe and the least understood organ in the human body. Neuroscientists with different backgrounds - medicine, psychology, electrical engineering, biomedical engineering, mechanical engineering, computer science, mathematics, physics and so on - aim to understand the brain. Furthermore, this research field has been receiving massive financial support, e.g.: the European Union has launched the Human Brain Project (HBP), budgeted in € 1-billion (HBP, 2015); and the US has launched the BRAIN (Brain Research through Advancing Innovative Neurotechnologies) initiative, budgeted in $100 million.
